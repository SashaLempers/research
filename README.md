# Research Manuscripts: Geometric Complexity Theory & Granular Physics

This repository serves as a public archive for independent research manuscripts covering topics in advanced algebra and theoretical physics.

---

## Research Areas

### 1. Geometric Complexity Theory (GCT)
The primary focus of this research lies in representation-theoretic obstructions in algebraic complexity. It introduces a proposed invariant, the **Schur-Rank Multiplication Tensor (SRMT)**, aimed at studying the structural rigidity of determinant orbit closures and bypassing restrictions established by the Bürgisser-Ikenmeyer-Panova (BIP) theorem.

### 2. Granular Physics
This section covers independent research in theoretical physics, specifically focusing on modeling non-local intrusion phenomena and complex physical interactions within granular media systems.
